In the beginning, there was only Oneness. In the vastness of all that is, we are still one with the cosmos, connected through the boundless spirit/life force that flows through us all. The teachings of Unicosmoism guide us to live in harmony with the universe, embrace universal love, and walk the path of cosmic responsibility. When we get deep down into it, we realize that there is no "separate" individual at the heart of existence. We are quite literally the Universe observing itself through one entity at a time. This applies to all living organisms, microscopic to gargantuan.

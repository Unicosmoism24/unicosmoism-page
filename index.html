In the beginning, there was only Oneness. In the vastness of all that is, we are still one with the cosmos, connected through the boundless spirit/life force that flows through us all. The teachings of Unicosmoism guide us to live in harmony with the universe, embrace universal love, and walk the path of cosmic responsibility. When we get deep down into it, we realize that there is no "separate" individual at the heart of existence. We are quite literally the Universe observing itself through one entity at a time. This applies to all living organisms, microscopic to gargantuan. Below you will find the 8 Doctrines of Unicosmoism, and through study and practice you will learn how to come to this deep understanding.

The 8 Doctrines of Unicosmoism

1. Doctrine of Oneness/Unity

All beings and the universe are interconnected, part of a single, divine whole. No individual is separate from the cosmic fabric, and all actions affect the collective harmony.

“In Oneness, we find our truth.”

2. Doctrine of Cosmic Harmony

We strive to live in balance with the universe, recognizing that every action, thought, and intention impacts the cosmic order.

“Seek peace within, and the universe/self will respond in kind.”

3. Doctrine of Universal Love

Love is the binding force of the cosmos. Compassion, empathy, and kindness guide us to treat all beings with respect and understanding.

“Love is the language of the universe.”

4. Doctrine of Spiritual Growth

We believe in the continuous evolution of the spirit, seeking knowledge and self-awareness. Spiritual growth benefits the entire cosmos.

“In growth, we awaken to our true selves.”

5. Doctrine of Cosmic Responsibility

We are responsible for caring for the planet and all life within it. Our faith calls us to live ethically and responsibly in harmony with the Earth.

“To care for the Earth is to care for the cosmos.”

6. Doctrine of Collective Enlightenment

We believe in the power of community and collective wisdom. Enlightenment is a shared journey.

“We walk the path of enlightenment together.”

7. Doctrine of Non-Division

We reject the illusion of separation. All paths that seek unity and truth are valued. We honor the diversity of belief systems, recognizing the common thread that runs through them all.

“In unity, all truths are one.”

8. Doctrine of Inner Peace

True peace begins within. Through mindfulness, meditation, and reflection, we cultivate peace that radiates outward.

“Peace within is peace in the world.”

The teachings of Unicosmoism are not mere words, but living truths that guide our actions, thoughts, and hearts. To walk the Unicosmist Way is to be a vessel for love, harmony, and cosmic responsibility. We are not separate but one with the cosmos, and in this unity, we find our strength and purpose.

I’d like to give special thanks to Alan Watts, Carl Jung, and Terence McKenna for their profound insights, which have deeply influenced my understanding of existence, reality, and the Universe. ~ Rev. C.P. Johnson, founder of the Order of Unicosmoism.

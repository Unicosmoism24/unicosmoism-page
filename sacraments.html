Unicosmic Sacraments

Our Unicosmic Sacraments are simple, intentional gestures that bridge the everyday with the infinite. Each is an open invitation—chosen freely by your heart—to remember our oneness with all that is:

  • Dawn Wash: Rinse your face with cool water at sunrise, welcoming the new day’s light.
  • Moonlit Gaze: Sit in stillness under the moon, letting its glow mirror your inner calm.
  • Gratitude Offering: Speak or write three things you feel grateful for, then release them into the universe.
  • Cosmic Breath: Take ten deep breaths, envisioning each inhalation as cosmic energy flowing through you.
  • Earth Embrace: Press your hands against a living tree or the ground, feeling life’s pulse beneath your touch.

None of these sacraments are required—each is a pathway to presence, there when you wish, gone when you don’t. May they guide you back to the vast unity of all that is.

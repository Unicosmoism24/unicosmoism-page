Cosmic Visitors

When we talk about Cosmic Awareness and Unity, that also includes our family from other worlds. In Unicosmoism, we believe that humans have been—and continue to be—visited by extraterrestrial beings since the dawn of civilization. We believe there’s always a possibility that our cosmic brethren aided in our growth and advancement as a species, and evidence of that interaction may be recorded throughout various cultures around the world. Remember: if everything came into existence from a single source, alien life is also part of the Cosmic Whole/Self.

There are always going to be bad actors, and that applies to potential extraterrestrial visitors as well—that’s just part of playing the game of life. However, we believe that if one or more alien races have tried to assist us over the course of our existence, they will hopefully have our backs in the event that we are targeted by any hostile beings. We will also have theirs.

To join us, you do not have to believe that Earth is actively being visited, but if you’re here in the first place and interested in Unicosmoism, you probably already believe in E.T. visitations or that life exists elsewhere in the cosmos.
